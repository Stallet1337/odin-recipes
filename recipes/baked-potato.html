Baked Potato Recipe

Description

Baked Potato makes a lovely lunch, dinner or even breakfast with a good filling.
Making a baked potato is not complicated and after this you will know just how to do it.

Ingredients (4 portions)

  • 4 Baked potatoes
  • Filling of your choice, we recommend either: cheese and beans, skagen stir fry, or chicken stir fry

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 225° or 200° hot air
  2. Wash the potatoes and poke them a few times with a fork
  3. Place them on the oven grid and bake until soft, approximately 45-60 minutes depending on the size of your potatoes
  4. Slice a cross almost through the potato and add the filling

  5. Enjoy your lunch, light dinner, appetizer or breakfast!
