Pasta Carbonara Recipe

Ingredients (4 portions)

  • 300g Spaghetti, or different pasta of your choice
  • 150g smoked pork
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 1/2 dl cream
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 garlic slice
  • 3 dl torn cheese, or any cheese of your preference
  • 2 cc black pepper
  • 4 egg yolks

Steps

  1. Boil the pasta
  2. Slice the pork into dices and fry in butter until crisp
  3. Whisk the cream, salt, garlic and half of the cheese
  4. Touch down the pork and cheese mixture into the boiled pasta and stir firmly to achieve a creamy consistency
  5. Sprinkle the remaining cheese and add pepper

  6. Serve the pasta carbonara with an egg yolk, Enjoy!
