Lasagna Recipe

Description

Lasagna, a staple of the Italian dynasty and its people in general. This wonderful dish can now be made at home following a few simple steps after this short description.
Uniting pasta, tomato sauce, minced meat and the immaculate flavor of cheese the lasagna will not only look good plated but will also fill all of your taste sensation desires.

Ingredients (4 portions)

  • 500g minced meat
  • 2 yellow onions
  • 2 garlic slices
  • 400g crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp tomato puree
  • 2 tbsp dried oregano
  • 1 cc black pepper
  • 1 dice vegetable broth
  • 9 lasagna plates
  • 3 tbsp wheat flour
  • 7 dl milk
  • 3 dl torn cheese, or any cheese of your preference
  • 1/2 tsp salt

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 225° or 200° hot air
  2. Dice and chop the onion and garlic. Gently fry in pan with butter
  3. Add the minced meat and fry while mixing, make sure it crumbles
  4. Add crushed tomatoes, tomato puree, oregano, pepper and the dice of vegetable broth. Let the sauce cook for five minutes
  5. Whisk the flour into half of the milk in a pot
  6. Pour the rest of the milk and let boil while whisking. Let the sauce cook for three minutes
  7. Touch down 2 1/2 dl of the cheese and taste with salt
  8. Repeat the following order until your form is filled: the minced meat sauce, the cheese sauce and finally the lasagna plate
  9. Finish off with sauce and sprinkle the remaining cheese on top
  10. Bake in the lower part of the oven for approximately 30 minutes

  11. Serve and enjoy a lovely dinner, lunch or even breakfast. You do you!
